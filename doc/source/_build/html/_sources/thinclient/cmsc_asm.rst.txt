CMSC ASM media
###############


Overview
==================


In WebRTC, the Thin Client sends out 4 resolutions (720p/360p/180p/90p)
and can receive up to 6 video streams simultaneously depending on the video layout
(one 720p stream and up to six 90p streams in stack/side-by-side view, and up to six 360p streams in grid view).
